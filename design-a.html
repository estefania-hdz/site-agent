In practice

How ECHT Onderwijs matched 35,000 professionals to teaching roles using skills, not just degrees

"Everday didn't just deliver a tool. They adapted their model to our world and helped us translate our data into a format education leaders can work with." Maurits Drenth Founder, ECHT Onderwijs

Problem

35,000 professionals wanted to enter teaching, but schools had no way to evaluate their skills. Formal qualifications were the only lens, leaving capable career switchers invisible to the system.

Solution

Everday's AI-native taxonomy, built on ESCO, was connected to ECHT's platform. Every candidate gets a skills passport showing how their abilities align with 7 core teaching profiles, from teacher to school leader.

Impact

Automatic matching to 7 teaching profiles. Schools see readiness and growth potential, not just degrees. Recognized by the Dutch Skills Ambassade as the national demonstration project for education.
